Intui was created in 2015 with the goal of fixing the simpler problems. Our apps and utilities are aimed to improve your productivity and allow you to spend more time focusing on the things you care about and less time worrying about the stuff you don’t.

All of our apps and utilities are open-sourced and released under the MIT License so that you can contribute and help us continue to build awesome software.
